Questions?

Booking FAQ

How far in advance should we book?

For popular weekends (spring, fall, Mardi Gras season), book 3-6 months ahead. These properties go fast for large groups.

What's the typical cost per person?

With 15-20 people splitting a villa, expect $100-200 per person per night. Often cheaper than hotels, with way more space.

Is there a minimum stay?

Most properties require a 2-3 night minimum on weekends. Perfect for a Friday-Sunday bach trip.

Can we have a party at the property?

These properties are specifically chosen because they can handle groups. Private pools, outdoor spaces, and no shared walls. Just be respectful of the space.

How do deposits and payments work?

Typically a 50% deposit to reserve, with the balance due 30-60 days before arrival. One person books, then Venmo request everyone else.
